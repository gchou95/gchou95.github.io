Glen Chou

Born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, I graduated from Foothill High School in 2013 and will graduate from UC Berkeley in 2017 with B.S. degrees in EECS and ME. Throughout my time at UC Berkeley, I have collaborated on research with Profs. Claire Tomlin and Anca Dragan. Apart from research, I am also passionate about teaching.

Aside from my academic interests, I like to practice abacus and mental math. In the past, I competed in state and international abacus and mental math competitions, so in my spare time I like to maintain those skills. I also love watching a wide variety of movies, playing obscure board games, hiking and exploring nature, and reading science fiction novels.
